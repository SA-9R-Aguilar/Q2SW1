My Favorite Characters


This is a list of animated characters that I resonate with. This list can give you an idea of my overall personality.


Image Cartoon Character Description
Aurora from Sleeping Beauty Aurora, also known as Briar Rose, is the protagonist of the 1959 film Sleeping Beauty, making her the third official member of the Disney Princess line-up. A daydreamer and a romantic, Aurora was one of the first characters I could relate to.
Belle from Beauty and the Beast Belle is the fifth member of the Disney Princess line-up. She debuted as the protagonist of the film Beauty and the Beast, released by Disney in 1991. Compassionate, imaginative, and a lover of books, Belle is easily my favorite Disney princess.
Darwin Watterson from The Amazing World of Gumball Darwin Raglan Caspian Ahab Poseidon Nicodemus Watterson III is one of the main characters of the Cartoon Network animated series, The Amazing World of Gumball. If you're wondering, he's a fish who grew legs with the power of friendship. Cute, sensitive, and sarcastic, Darwin is my spirit animal.
Derpy from My Little Pony Derpy, also known as Muffins, is a pegasus pony and one of the background characters in the Hasbro series, My Little Pony. Growing up, I was very clumsy, and Derpy is usually seen making clumsy mistakes and unintended troubles, which is why I can relate to her. I'm not as cute as Derpy when I trip, though.
Eeyore from Winnie The Pooh Eeyore is an old, stuffed donkey from the Disney film and series franchise of Winnie the Pooh. Eeyore is usually gloomy and pessimistic, which is why my family tells me that I'm an Eeyore. Despite this, Eeyore still loves his friends and will always be there for them.
